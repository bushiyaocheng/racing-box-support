RACING BOX / PRIVACY

Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

Overview

Racing Box is developed by YAO CHENG. The app is designed to provide motorsport schedules, standings, results, weather signals, reminders, and Home Screen widgets without requiring an account.

Information we do not collect

Racing Box does not ask for your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location, or advertising identifier. It does not include third-party advertising, behavioral analytics, or cross-app tracking technologies.

App settings and cached data

Your language, appearance, reminder selections, favorite driver and team, widget preferences, and cached race data are stored locally on your device or in the app’s shared container so that the app and its widgets can work together. Racing Box does not operate an account system or upload these preferences to a developer-controlled account database.

You can remove locally stored app data by deleting Racing Box from your device. Purchase records associated with your Apple Account are controlled by Apple and are not removed when the app is deleted.

Network data providers

To retrieve schedules, standings, results, and circuit weather, Racing Box sends requests to race-data and weather providers, including OpenF1, Jolpica, and Open-Meteo. These requests concern public race information and circuit coordinates, not your device’s location.

These providers may receive and retain standard technical information such as an IP address, request time, requested URL, circuit coordinates, and user-agent information when a request is made. Open-Meteo states that its API logs are deleted after 90 days. Racing Box does not receive or store provider logs. Provider logs are governed by each provider’s policies and may be used to operate, secure, and protect their services; Racing Box does not use them for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Provider information: OpenF1, Jolpica, and Open-Meteo terms. Attribution and licensing links are collected on the Data & Licenses page.

Notifications

If you enable race reminders, Racing Box requests notification permission and schedules reminders locally on your device. The app does not use reminders to build an advertising profile.

Purchases

Racing Box Pro purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Racing Box can receive transaction and entitlement status needed to unlock purchased features, but it does not receive your full payment-card details. Apple’s processing is governed by Apple’s privacy policy and media-services terms.

Children

Racing Box is a general-audience motorsport utility and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes and contact

This policy may be updated if Racing Box’s features or data practices change. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are made.

Questions about this policy can be sent to bushiyaocheng@gmail.com.

RACING BOX / 隐私

隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 21 日

概述

Racing Box 由 YAO CHENG 开发。本 App 无需注册账户，即可提供赛车赛程、排名、比赛结果、天气信号、比赛提醒和 iPhone 桌面小组件。

我们不会收集的信息

Racing Box 不会要求你提供姓名、电子邮箱、联系人、照片、精确位置或广告标识符，也不包含第三方广告、行为分析或跨 App 跟踪技术。

App 设置与缓存数据

你的语言、外观、提醒选项、最爱车手和车队、小组件偏好以及缓存的比赛数据，会保存在设备本地或 App 的共享容器中，以便 App 与小组件共同工作。Racing Box 不提供账户系统，也不会把这些偏好上传到开发者控制的账户数据库。

你可以通过从设备删除 Racing Box 来清除本地 App 数据。与 Apple 账户关联的购买记录由 Apple 管理，删除 App 不会清除这些记录。

网络数据服务

为了获取赛程、排名、比赛结果和赛道天气，Racing Box 会向 OpenF1、Jolpica、Open-Meteo 等比赛与天气数据服务发送请求。请求内容是公开比赛信息和赛道坐标，不是你设备的当前位置。

这些服务可能在收到请求时获取并保留 IP 地址、请求时间、请求网址、赛道坐标、用户代理等标准技术信息。Open-Meteo 声明其 API 日志会在 90 天后删除。Racing Box 不会收到或保存服务商日志；服务商可能依据各自政策使用日志来运行、保护及防止滥用其服务，Racing Box 不会将这些信息用于广告或跨 App 跟踪。

服务商信息：OpenF1、Jolpica、Open-Meteo 条款。相关署名和许可链接集中列于数据来源与授权页面。

通知

如果你开启比赛提醒，Racing Box 会请求通知权限，并在设备本地安排提醒。本 App 不会使用提醒数据建立广告画像。

购买

Racing Box Pro 购买由 Apple 通过 StoreKit 处理。Racing Box 可以获得解锁已购功能所需的交易与权益状态，但不会获得你的完整银行卡或支付卡信息。Apple 对相关信息的处理受 Apple 隐私政策及媒体服务条款约束。

儿童隐私

Racing Box 是面向普通用户的赛车工具，不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

政策更新与联系

如果 Racing Box 的功能或数据处理方式发生变化，本政策可能相应更新。发生重大变化时，我们会修改上方的生效日期。

如对本政策有疑问，请发送邮件至 bushiyaocheng@gmail.com。
