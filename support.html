RACING BOX / SUPPORT

Support

Help with the app, Home Screen widgets, race data, reminders, and purchases.

Contact

Email bushiyaocheng@gmail.com and include your device model, iOS version, Racing Box version, and a short description of the issue. Screenshots are helpful when the issue is visual.

Home Screen widgets

  1. Touch and hold an empty area of the Home Screen until the apps jiggle.
  2. Tap Edit, then Add Widget.
  3. Search for Racing Box, choose a size, and tap Add Widget.

If a widget is not current, open Racing Box once, allow the latest data to load, then return to the Home Screen. iOS controls the exact widget refresh schedule.

Race data and weather

Racing Box uses internet data providers for schedules, standings, results, and circuit weather. Temporary provider or network interruptions can delay an update. The app may show bundled or cached data while it retries.

Racing Box does not provide official live timing and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any racing series, team, driver, or circuit.

Racing Box Pro and purchases

  • Prices are supplied by the App Store for your Apple Account storefront.
  • To restore an earlier purchase, open Settings → Racing Box Pro → Restore Purchases.
  • Monthly subscriptions can be managed from Racing Box Settings or your Apple Account subscription settings.
  • Racing Box never receives your full payment-card information.

RACING BOX / 技术支持

技术支持

获取 App、桌面小组件、比赛数据、提醒与购买相关帮助。

联系我们

请发送邮件至 bushiyaocheng@gmail.com，并附上设备型号、iOS 版本、Racing Box 版本及问题描述。如属于界面问题，建议同时提供截图。

添加桌面小组件

  1. 长按主屏幕空白区域，直到 App 图标开始抖动。
  2. 轻点编辑，然后选择添加小组件。
  3. 搜索 Racing Box，选择尺寸并轻点添加小组件。

如果组件数据没有及时更新，请先打开一次 Racing Box，等待最新数据载入后再返回主屏幕。组件的具体刷新时间由 iOS 系统控制。

比赛数据与天气

Racing Box 通过互联网数据服务获取赛程、排名、比赛结果和赛道天气。数据服务或网络发生短暂中断时，更新可能延迟，App 会在重试期间显示内置或缓存数据。

Racing Box 不提供官方实时计时。本 App 为独立开发的非官方赛车工具，不代表、隶属于或获得任何赛事组织、车队、车手或赛道的认可。

Racing Box Pro 与购买

  • 商品价格由 App Store 根据你的 Apple 账户商店地区提供。
  • 恢复已有购买：打开设置 → Racing Box Pro → 恢复购买。
  • 月度订阅可以在 Racing Box 设置页或 Apple 账户的订阅设置中管理。
  • Racing Box 不会获取你的完整银行卡或支付卡信息。
