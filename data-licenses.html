Race and weather data sources, attribution, and independence notice.

How data is used

Racing Box retrieves public race schedules, session information, standings, results, and circuit weather from third-party services. The underlying information remains free to view inside the app. Racing Box Pro only unlocks additional Home Screen widget presentations and personalization.

Access to and reuse of provider material is governed by each provider’s current terms and licences. Listing a provider here does not mean that the provider sponsors or endorses Racing Box.

Processing, caching, and corrections

Racing Box may normalize provider field names, translate labels, convert session times to the device’s selected time zone, calculate display-only countdowns or summaries, and cache recent responses for app and widget reliability. Bundled fallback data may appear during temporary provider outages.

Provider data can be delayed, incomplete, or corrected after publication. Racing Box does not provide official live timing. Verify time-sensitive information with the relevant official organizer.

Trademarks and independence

All third-party names, trademarks, logos, and visual identities remain the property of their respective owners. Their appearance does not imply endorsement, and Racing Box claims no ownership of them.

处理、缓存与修正

为了展示和组件稳定性，Racing Box 可能统一数据字段名称、翻译标签、将比赛时间转换为设备选择的时区、计算仅用于显示的倒计时或摘要，并缓存近期响应。数据服务暂时不可用时，App 可能显示内置备用数据。

服务商数据可能延迟、缺失或在发布后被修正。Racing Box 不提供官方实时计时；对时间敏感的信息，请向相关官方组织核实。
